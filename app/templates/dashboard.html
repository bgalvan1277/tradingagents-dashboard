Chief Portfolio Manager

TradingAgents is a proprietary multi-agent intelligence system that deploys autonomous AI analysts across four specialized research verticals: technical analysis, sentiment quantification, news signal processing, and fundamental valuation modeling. Each agent operates independently, generating isolated research before entering a structured adversarial debate protocol where competing bull and bear advocates stress-test every thesis.

A dedicated Research Director synthesizes the debate outcomes, while a three-tier risk assessment panel (aggressive, conservative, and neutral) evaluates exposure parameters. The entire pipeline converges on my desk, where I issue the final portfolio directive. No single point of failure. No emotional bias. Just systematic, machine-augmented conviction.
