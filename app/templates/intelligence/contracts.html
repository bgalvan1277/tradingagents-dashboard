Intelligence Value

Federal contract awards represent predictable, multi-year revenue streams that directly impact earnings forecasts. Monitoring USASpending.gov reveals which companies are winning defense, intelligence, and civilian agency work. For companies like Palantir (PLTR), Booz Allen (BAH), and Leidos (LDOS), contract announcements can move the stock significantly. Key signals include: new award notifications, contract modifications (expansions or reductions), protest outcomes, and indefinite delivery/indefinite quantity (IDIQ) ceiling increases. The USASpending API provides programmatic access to every transaction above the micro-purchase threshold.
