SEC EDGAR is the primary source for all publicly filed corporate disclosures. Form 4 filings reveal insider buys and sells within 2 business days of the transaction. 13F filings (quarterly) show institutional holdings above $100M, revealing how hedge funds and pension funds are positioning. 8-K filings capture material events: M&A announcements, leadership changes, earnings restatements, and material agreements. 10-K/10-Q annual and quarterly reports provide the fundamental data backbone.
