Intelligence Value

FDA decisions are among the highest-impact binary events in equity markets. A single PDUFA date can move a biotech stock 50-200% in either direction. PDUFA dates (Prescription Drug User Fee Act) are the FDA's target action dates for drug applications. AdCom meetings (Advisory Committee) often precede PDUFA decisions and provide early directional signals. Complete Response Letters (CRLs) indicate rejection and typically crash the stock. Tracking the FDA pipeline provides the intelligence necessary to position ahead of these catalysts or avoid exposure during high-volatility windows.
