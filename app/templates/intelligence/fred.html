Intelligence Value

FRED provides the quantitative backbone for macroeconomic analysis. Critical series include: Federal Funds Rate (monetary policy direction), CPI (inflation trajectory), 10Y-2Y Treasury Spread (yield curve inversion/recession indicator), Unemployment Rate (labor market health), and M2 Money Supply (liquidity conditions). The FRED API (free with registration) supports programmatic access to all 800,000+ series with historical data going back decades. Integration into the agent pipeline would allow automated macro-regime detection and sector rotation signals.
